1. Go to https://beta.elevenlabs.io/
2. Make an account and select the Rachel voice.
3. Generate lines as normal.
4. Download Audacity. Set the project rate (hz) to 16000.
5. Apply reverb, the settings are this: 
	Room size: 40
	Pre-delay: 48
	Reverberance: 70
	Damping: 57
	Tone low: 84
	Tone High: 85
	Wet gain (dB): 3
	Dray gain (dB): -3
	Stereo Width (%): 32
	Wet only: no
6. Export as .ogg at 10 quality.
7. Done.
